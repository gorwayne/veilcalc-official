About VeilCalc

VeilCalc is a unique local notepad app disguised as a standard calculator, perfectly hiding your private notes. Simply enter your custom digital password to unlock the notepad functionality and record, view, and manage your personal thoughts anytime. All data is stored locally without internet connection, ensuring privacy security and making it an ideal choice for modern digital privacy protection.
